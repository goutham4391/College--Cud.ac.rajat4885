Careers

At Canadian University Dubai, we are committed to providing our students with the resources necessary to fulfil their learning potential.

Founded on the premise of academic excellence and guided by the highest educational and corporate ethics, we provide rich opportunities in education, research, and professional development that adds value to the personal and professional lives of our students, staff, and faculty members, as well as the communities in which we serve.

Accredited by the Ministry of Education – High Education Affairs, our enthusiastic team delivers a recognized leading-edge curriculum tailored to a quality Canadian education experience, creating opportunities for students to pursue their academic goals and career aspirations in both Canada and abroad.

Our recently expanded City Walk campus presents an ideal learning environment conducive to entrepreneurship, creativity, and innovation, where students benefit from state-of-the-art facilities in the heart of Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

If you’re looking to join a diverse, reputable, and dynamic team, we are ready for you!
